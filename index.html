How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Bring your own API key for complete control over costs and privacy. No subscriptions, no hidden fees.

1

Get an API Key

Sign up with OpenAI or Google Gemini and generate an API key. Both offer generous free tiers to get started.

2

Add It to the App

Paste your key into Settings. It's stored securely in your device's Keychain and never leaves your phone.

3

Choose Your Mode

Translate conversations in real time or dive into interactive lessons with your AI tutor.

4

Track Your Growth

Review transcripts, study flashcards, and watch your mastery levels rise over time.
